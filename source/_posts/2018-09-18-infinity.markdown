### Infinity
### ref https://stackoverflow.com/questions/5778295/how-to-express-infinity-in-ruby
PositiveInfinity = +1.0/0.0 
=> Infinity

NegativeInfinity = -1.0/0.0 
=> -Infinity

CompleteInfinity = NegativeInfinity..PositiveInfinity
=> -Infinity..Infinity
